    IIIT Raichur


    FOUNDATION

    The Indian Institute of Information technology, Raichur, Karnataka is one of the new IIITs proposed by the Ministry of Human Resource Development, Government of India.IIIT Raichur has initiated its functioning in Hyderabad in August 2019, under the mentorship of IIT Hyderabad. IIIT Raichur has started its first batch in the academic year 2019-20 with a strength of 30 students in the department of computer science and engineering.
